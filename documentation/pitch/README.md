## Pitch Template Structure

Every pitch should follow this structure:

1. **The Hook** (30 seconds)
   - Problem statement that resonates
   - "I built AI that creates OTHER AI agents"

2. **The Problem** (2 minutes)
   - Real example they've experienced
   - Cost of the problem (time + money)

3. **The Solution** (5 minutes)
   - How QUAD solves it differently
   - Live demo or visual diagram

4. **The Competitive Advantage** (3 minutes)
   - Why Microsoft/competitors can't copy
   - Patent protection
   - Technical breakthrough (0% error rate)

5. **The Market Opportunity** (2 minutes)
   - Total addressable market
   - Current traction
   - 5-year revenue projection

6. **The Ask** (2 minutes)
   - What you want (feedback, pilot, investment)
   - Next steps
   - Timeline
